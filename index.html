How to roll out a lightweight focus report in one afternoon

Teams often fail with analytics because the rollout is heavier than the product. Start with one squad, connect calendar access, and agree on three metrics that matter for the next sprint review.

In most setups, the first useful report appears after four working days. Managers use it to spot meeting creep; ICs use it to protect two focus blocks per day.

  • Connect Google Calendar or Outlook in Settings → Integrations.
  • Invite up to 8 people on the Starter plan.
  • Pick a report template: Engineering, Design, or Customer Success.
  • Schedule the digest for Thursday 4:00 PM local time.
